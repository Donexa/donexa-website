Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2024

1. Introduction

Donexa ("we," "our," or "us") operates the OpenCarenet CHE application. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, manage, and protect your information when you use our mobile application, particularly regarding the handling of sensitive health information.

2. Information Collection and Use

OpenCarenet CHE is designed for Community Health Workers to collect Electronic Health Records (EHR) and clinic management data. The app collects:

  • Patient Data: Demographics, medical history, and health metrics required for primary care tracking.
  • Geolocation Data: Location data is collected to map health trends, disease outbreaks, and verify the coverage areas of community health workers.
  • Device Information: Data necessary for the offline functionality and synchronization of the app.

3. Data Storage, Offline Functionality, and Synchronization

Due to the nature of operating in areas with limited internet connectivity, OpenCarenet CHE stores patient data locally on your device in a secure, encrypted format. Once an internet connection is established, this data is securely aggregated and synchronized with centralized databases, specifically utilizing interoperability with DHIS2 and OpenClinic GA.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade patient data. Data collected via the app is only transmitted to the authorized national or organizational health information systems (such as DHIS2) configured by your deploying health organization.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us with health data. We employ industry-standard security measures, including data encryption both at rest (on the device) and in transit (during synchronization), to protect against unauthorized access or alteration of data.
